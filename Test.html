March 29, 2021
Good Monday morning, Walter readers. Today is March 29, 2021, the last Monday of the month. Thirty-nine years ago today, a University of North Carolina freshman named Michael Jordan knocked down the go-ahead jump shot with 15 seconds left in the National Championship against Georgetown. It was Jordan’s first big moment on a national stage, and certainly a sign of things to come.

There was plenty of March Madness taking place over this weekend, but let’s first focus on the headlines you may have missed.
